Immich Wallpaper

Rotates your desktop wallpaper from your self-hosted Immich library. Sits quietly in your menu bar.

Available for macOS & Linux

Download

Single image or collage — fill your screen with one shot or a mosaic that sizes itself to each image's shape.

Presets — morning photos, holiday snaps, only the dog. Save setups and switch between them in one click.

macOS & Linux — lives quietly in your menu bar. You'll forget it's there until your wallpaper changes.
